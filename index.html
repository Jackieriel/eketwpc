Who We Are

The Eket WordPress Community is a vibrant part of the global WordPress Community network. It brings together a diverse group of enthusiasts, including WordPress users, developers, freelancers, business owners, bloggers, content writers, designers, and anyone eager to learn more about this powerful CMS.

Since its inauguration in June 2024, the community has become a hub for sharing knowledge, networking, and collaborating on various projects and initiatives. Whether you're a seasoned WordPress expert or just starting out, the Eket WordPress Community offers a welcoming space to connect, grow, and contribute to the WordPress ecosystem.

Our Values

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone, regardless of background or experience level. Our community is a safe and supportive space where all voices are heard and respected.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together. Through teamwork and shared knowledge, we achieve more and build stronger, more impactful projects.

Innovation

We are committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible with WordPress. By embracing creativity and new ideas, we continuously evolve and improve our skills and contributions.

Our Goals and Purpose

  • Learn

    Improve our WordPress skills through workshops, tutorials, and code reviews.

  • Network

    Connect with other developers, freelancers, bloggers, content writers, and business owners who use WordPress.
